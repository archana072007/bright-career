Career Domains

IT & Software

Roles: Software Developer, Data Analyst, AI Engineer

Skills: Programming, Logical Thinking

IT & Technology

Overview: This domain focuses on software development, data processing, and modern technologies.

Career Roles: Software Developer, Data Analyst, AI Engineer, Cyber Security Analyst

Required Qualification: B.E / B.Tech / BCA / MCA or certification courses

Course Duration: 3–4 years (degree) or 6–12 months (certification)

Job Opportunities: IT companies, startups, MNCs, government IT sectors

Starting Salary: ₹3 – ₹6 LPA (approx)

Healthcare

Roles: Doctor, Nurse, Pharmacist

Skills: Medical Knowledge, Communication

Healthcare & Life Sciences

Overview: Healthcare focuses on medical treatment, patient care, and health services.

Career Roles: Doctor, Nurse, Pharmacist, Lab Technician, Physiotherapist

Required Qualification: MBBS, B.Sc Nursing, B.Pharm, D.Pharm

Course Duration: 3–5.5 years

Job Opportunities: Hospitals, clinics, laboratories

Starting Salary: ₹2.5 – ₹6 LPA (approx)

Government Sector

Roles: IAS, Banking Officer, Defence

Skills: Discipline, Leadership

Government & Public Sector

Overview: Government jobs provide public service, stability, and career growth.

Career Roles: IAS, IPS, Banking Officer, Defence Services

Required Qualification: Graduation + competitive exams

Course Duration: 3 years degree + exam preparation

Job Opportunities: Central and state government departments

Starting Salary: ₹4 – ₹7 LPA (approx)

How to Choose a Career

  1. Identify your interests
  2. Analyze your skills
  3. Check qualifications
  4. Understand job roles
  5. Choose based on passion and demand

Law & Legal Services

Overview: The law domain focuses on legal systems, justice, and protection of rights.

Career Roles: Lawyer, Advocate, Legal Advisor, Corporate Lawyer, Judge (through exams)

Required Qualification: 3-year LLB (after graduation) or 5-year integrated LLB (after 12th)

Course Duration: 3–5 years

Job Opportunities: Courts, law firms, corporate companies, government legal departments

Starting Salary: ₹3 – ₹6 LPA (approx)

Fashion Designing

Overview: Fashion designing involves creating clothing, accessories, and lifestyle products.

Career Roles: Fashion Designer, Apparel Designer, Fashion Stylist, Textile Designer

Required Qualification: Diploma or degree in fashion designing

Course Duration: 1–4 years

Job Opportunities: Fashion houses, textile industries, export houses, self-employment

Starting Salary: ₹2 – ₹5 LPA (approx)

Aviation & Air Hostess

Overview: The aviation domain focuses on airline services, passenger safety, and travel operations.

Career Roles: Air Hostess, Cabin Crew, Ground Staff, Airport Operations Executive

Required Qualification: 12th pass + cabin crew training certification

Course Duration: 6 months – 1 year

Job Opportunities: Airlines, airports, aviation service companies

Starting Salary: ₹4 – ₹7 LPA (approx)

Content Creator & Digital Media

Overview: This domain focuses on creating digital content for online platforms.

Career Roles: YouTuber, Blogger, Social Media Influencer, Content Writer, Video Creator

Required Qualification: No mandatory degree; skills and creativity are important

Course Duration: 3–12 months (skill-based learning)

Job Opportunities: Social media platforms, digital agencies, freelancing

Starting Salary: ₹2 – ₹6 LPA (approx, depends on audience and engagement)
